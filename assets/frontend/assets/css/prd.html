  • How to order

    • These tiles are sold by the box and ordering is easy. 

      Simply enter how many square metres you’ll need and we’ll tell you how many boxes are required to cover this area. We’ll always round this up to the nearest full box. 

      You can also order by the box if you know how many you'll need.
       

      Either way, please remember to include 10% extra to cover cuts and wastage.

    Q: If I use Heritage star for my kitchen floor, do you have a matching or similar shade like the background of the heritage star, meaning without a pattern so I could use it above the worktops including the splashback area? I'm not experienced but I would like to try to tile the floor myself, would you say heritage star would be easy to cut by a hand tile cutter, meaning not just cutting a 45 x 45 tile on scored lines but cutting through the tile as if you had a 22 x 22 sample size and needed to cut it making sure the thin top patterned layer of the tile is cut smoothly? Ot will I need a diamond tile cutter? ......... or would they even be possible to cut without power tools, by scribing and using a hand tool called an 'amazing' tile and glass cutter? ...... I think I've watched too many YouTube videos! LOL
    Answers (1)
    Porcelain Superstore
    11/16/20
    A: Hi Dee
    Thanks for getting in touch. To answer your queries:
    1. Yes, you can cut these tiles using either a 'manual tile cutter' for straight cuts or an electric 'wet tile cutter' for more awkward shaped cuts. Both should give you a good quality cut - it's best to use a new, high quality blade or wheel to obtain the best cut. You probably won't be able to use the "Amazing Tile and Glass Cutter" to cut these tiles.
    2. For the wall tiles, we'd recommend a tile such as Harmony Stone or Dorset Ivory, which will co-ordinate with the background of Heritage Star and have a similar vintage style.
    When it comes to laying the floor tiles, it's vital to make sure your current floor is as level and stable as possible.
    If you have any further queries, please email us on help@porcelainsuperstore.co.uk and we'll be able to help
    Kind regards, Porcelain Superstore
